Architect of the Month

Zaha Hadid

Iraq · United Kingdom — b.1950

Dame Zaha Hadid was one of the world's most influential architects, celebrated for her bold, futuristic forms and groundbreaking approach to design. Her work redefined modern architecture through fluid geometries and innovative engineering.

Some of her iconic projects include the Heydar Aliyev Center, the London Aquatics Centre, and the Guangzhou Opera House.
